## Two Contrasting Motifs

### Motif 1: "Air Theme" (Crespin - Operatic)
**Character:** Soaring, elegant, operatic, air-like
**Pattern:** G-B-D-G-A (ascending, Lydian)
**Measures:** 1-20, 81-90
**Style:** Legato, tenuto, elegant phrasing

### Motif 2: "Baroque Dance" (Franklin - Soul/R&B)
**Character:** Soulful, dance-like, rhythmic, syncopated
**Pattern:** E-G-B-E-D-B-G (dance-like, modal)
**Measures:** 21-40 (Violin II), 25-40 (Violin I)
**Style:** Rhythmic, expressive, with grace notes

**Contrast:**
- Motif 1: Operatic, soaring, elegant (Crespin)
- Motif 2: Soulful, dance-like, rhythmic (Franklin)
- Together: Create a sophisticated baroque jazz-pop duet
