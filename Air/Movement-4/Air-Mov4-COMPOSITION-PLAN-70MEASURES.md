# Air Movement 4 - Composition Plan (70 Measures)

## Executive Summary
Creating a 70-measure baroque jazz-pop string quartet movement with two strong evolving motifs. The piece will be beautiful, air-like, ethereal, and flowing - less funky, more elegant and airy.

---

## Two Strong Motifs

### Motif 1 - "Air Theme" (Ascending, Flowing, Ethereal)
**Measures 1-4:** G4-B4-D5-G5-A5 (ascending, legato, air-like)
- **Character:** Flowing, ethereal, air-like
- **Harmony:** G major (I)
- **Development:**
  - Sequencing: Measures 5-8, 25-28, 49-52
  - Inversion: Measures 13-16, 41-44
  - Augmentation: Measures 33-36
  - Fragmentation: Measures 57-60, 65-68

### Motif 2 - "Baroque Dance" (Elegant, Dance-like)
**Measures 17-20:** E4-G4-B4-E5-D5-B4-G4 (dance-like, with grace notes)
- **Character:** Elegant, baroque-inflected, dance-like
- **Harmony:** Em7-A7-D7 (vi-ii-V)
- **Development:**
  - Sequencing: Measures 21-24, 37-40, 53-56
  - Inversion: Measures 29-32, 45-48
  - Diminution: Measures 61-64
  - Fragmentation: Measures 69-70
