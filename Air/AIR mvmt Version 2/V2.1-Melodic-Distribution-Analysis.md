## SPECIFIC REVISION SUGGESTIONS

### Measures 9-16: Violin II Countermelody
- **Violin II:** Independent countermelody (not doubling Violin I)
- **Pattern:** Flowing eighth notes, baroque-inflected
- **Register:** G4-D5 (comfortable, expressive)
- **Dynamic:** `mp` (supporting but independent)

### Measures 17-24: Violin II Takes Melody
- **Violin II:** Primary melody (Motif 1 variation)
- **Violin I:** Countermelody or supporting harmony
- **Viola:** Light counterpoint
- **Cello:** Bass foundation

### Measures 25-32: Viola Featured
- **Viola:** Featured melodic passage
- **Register:** C-string (C3-G3) for warmth and depth
- **Pattern:** Flowing melody, Motif 2 variation
- **Dynamic:** `mp` → `mf` (featured)
- **Marking:** "espressivo", "sul C"

### Measures 33-40: Cello Melodic
- **Cello:** Featured melodic passage in tenor register
- **Register:** C3-G4 (tenor register - idiomatic)
- **Pattern:** Motif 1 or Motif 2 variation
- **Technique:** Arco (not pizzicato)
- **Dynamic:** `mp` → `mf` (featured)
- **Marking:** "espressivo"
