## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Air-Specific Techniques

**Flautando (Flute-like):**
- Use in sparse sections (m53-56, 61-64, 69-72)
- Creates light, airy timbre
- Mark: "flautando" or "sul tasto" (over fingerboard)

**Brushed Textures:**
- Light pizzicato (not aggressive)
- Featherlight syncopations
- Mark: "leggiero" (light)

**Translucent Timbres:**
- Open voicings
- Upper extensions (9ths, 11ths, 13ths)
- Wide spacing

**Elegant Registral Motion:**
- Gliding between registers
- Quick-shifting textures
- Circulating melodic material
