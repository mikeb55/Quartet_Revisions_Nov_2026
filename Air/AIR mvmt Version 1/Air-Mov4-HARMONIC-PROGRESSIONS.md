# Air Movement 4 - Harmonic Progressions
## Burt Bacharach / Quincy Jones / French Modern Jazz Style

---

## Harmonic Style Reference

### Burt Bacharach Characteristics
- Extended chords: Maj7, Min7, Dom9, Maj9, Min9, 11ths, 13ths
- Chromatic voice leading
- Unexpected modulations
- Sophisticated ii-V-I with substitutions
- Major 7th chords for elegance
- Diminished chords for color

### Quincy Jones Characteristics
- Rich chord voicings
- Modal interchange (parallel major/minor)
- Secondary dominants (V/V, V/vi, V/ii, V/IV)
- Smooth voice leading
- Extended chords with color tones

### French Modern Jazz Characteristics
- Modal jazz (Lydian, Mixolydian, Dorian)
- Quartal/quintal harmony (stacked 4ths/5ths)
- Impressionist colors (Debussy/Ravel influence)
- Whole-tone scales
- Pentatonic scales
- Extended chords

## Chord Voicing Principles

### String Quartet Voicing
- **Violin I:** Melody (top voice)
- **Violin II:** Upper harmony (3rd, 7th, 9th)
- **Viola:** Middle harmony (5th, 7th, 9th, 11th)
- **Cello:** Bass (root, 3rd, 5th) or walking bass

### Extended Chord Voicings
- **Maj9:** Root (Cello), 3rd (Viola), 5th (Violin II), 7th (Viola), 9th (Violin I)
- **Min11:** Root (Cello), 3rd (Viola), 5th (Violin II), 7th (Viola), 9th (Violin II), 11th (Violin I)
- **Dom13:** Root (Cello), 3rd (Viola), 7th (Violin II), 9th (Violin II), 13th (Violin I)

### Quartal Voicings (French Modern Jazz)
- Stacked 4ths: C-F-Bb-Eb (Viola, Violin II, Violin I)
- Creates modern, open sound
- Air-like, ethereal quality

## Implementation Notes

1. **Extended Chords:** Use 9ths, 11ths, 13ths throughout
2. **Modal Interchange:** Mix parallel major/minor (G major ↔ G minor)
3. **Secondary Dominants:** V/V, V/vi, V/ii, V/IV for sophistication
4. **Tritone Substitutions:** bII7 for V7 (Ab7 for D7)
5. **Chromatic Voice Leading:** Smooth, elegant progressions
6. **Quartal Harmony:** Stacked 4ths for modern color
7. **Suspended Chords:** 9sus4, 13sus4 for French modern jazz flavor
8. **Lydian Colors:** #11 for brightness and air-like quality
