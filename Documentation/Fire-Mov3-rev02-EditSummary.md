## Fire Theme Reinforcement

### Techniques Applied:
1. **Dynamic Contrasts**: Wide range from `ppp` to `fff` creates fire's breathing quality
2. **Tempo Modifications**: Accelerando, stringendo create intensifying fire effect
3. **Expressive Markings**: 
   - **con fuoco** = fire ignition
   - **furioso** = raging fire
   - **molto agitato** = agitated flames
   - **morendo/smorzando** = fire extinguishing
   - **con sordino** = muted, dying embers
4. **Articulations**: Tenuto on sustained notes = glowing embers
5. **Fermata**: Final pause = dramatic conclusion
